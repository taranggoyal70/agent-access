## Agent Access commitments

- Restrict the initial connection to the approved HTTPS staging origin.
- Permit only reviewed read-only capabilities in Shadow Mode.
- Encrypt the upstream staging credential and never expose it to the external agent.
- Produce redacted, signed Execution Receipts.
- Revoke the connection promptly on request.
- Provide founder-led onboarding and support during the pilot.

## Design Partner commitments

- Provide one implementation owner and a staging contract.
- Complete onboarding and one real external-agent execution.
- Use the workflow for four consecutive weeks when technically viable.
- Participate in a weekly 20-minute feedback session.
- Permit Agent Access to report aggregated, non-sensitive execution counts.

## Success criteria

- The external agent completes the target workflow without receiving the upstream credential.
- At least 25 verified upstream executions occur during the pilot.
- No mutating capability is exposed.
- The Design Partner makes a clear continue, pay, or stop decision at review.
